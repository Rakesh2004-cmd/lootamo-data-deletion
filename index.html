User Data Deletion Instructions – Lootamo

At Lootamo, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data.

If you want your personal data deleted from our system, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to: vap.globalia@gmail.com

with the subject line:
“Lootamo – Data Deletion Request”

and include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address used with Lootamo or Facebook Login
  • Your Facebook profile ID (if available)

What Happens Next

After receiving your request:

  • We will verify your identity
  • We will delete your personal data from our systems
  • We will confirm deletion by email within 7 business days
